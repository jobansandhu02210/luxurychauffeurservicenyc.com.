Service Areas

Luxury chauffeur service throughout New York City, the Tri-State area, and beyond. Wherever you need to go, we'll get you there in style.

NYC Boroughs

Serving All Five Boroughs

From the heart of Manhattan to the far reaches of Staten Island, our chauffeurs know every neighborhood.

Manhattan

As the business and cultural heart of New York City, Manhattan is where we do most of our work. Our chauffeurs navigate the busy streets of Midtown, the financial corridors of Lower Manhattan, and the elegant neighborhoods of the Upper East and West Sides with ease.

Popular destinations:

  • Midtown (Times Square, Rockefeller Center, Grand Central)
  • Financial District (Wall Street, World Trade Center)
  • Upper East Side (Museum Mile, Park Avenue)
  • Upper West Side (Lincoln Center, Central Park West)
  • Chelsea, SoHo, Tribeca, Greenwich Village
  • Harlem and Washington Heights

Brooklyn

Brooklyn's diverse neighborhoods offer everything from waterfront luxury to artistic enclaves. We provide reliable service throughout the borough, whether you're heading to a meeting in DUMBO or an event in Park Slope.

Popular destinations:

  • DUMBO and Brooklyn Heights
  • Williamsburg and Greenpoint
  • Park Slope and Prospect Heights
  • Downtown Brooklyn and Fort Greene
  • Bay Ridge, Bensonhurst, Brighton Beach
  • Barclays Center and Atlantic Terminal

Queens

Home to both JFK and LaGuardia airports, Queens is a major hub for our airport transfer services. We also serve the borough's diverse neighborhoods for all transportation needs.

Popular destinations:

  • Long Island City and Astoria
  • Flushing and Corona (Citi Field, USTA)
  • Forest Hills and Rego Park
  • Jamaica and JFK Airport area
  • LaGuardia Airport and surrounding areas
  • Rockaway Beach and Howard Beach

The Bronx

From the upscale neighborhoods of Riverdale to the excitement of Yankee Stadium, we provide comprehensive service throughout the Bronx.

Popular destinations:

  • Riverdale and Fieldston
  • Pelham Bay and City Island
  • Fordham and the Bronx Zoo area
  • Yankee Stadium and the Grand Concourse
  • Co-op City and Throgs Neck
  • Botanical Garden area

Staten Island

We provide service to and from Staten Island, including connections to the ferry terminal and all major neighborhoods.

Popular destinations:

  • St. George (Ferry Terminal area)
  • Todt Hill and Grymes Hill
  • Great Kills and New Dorp
  • Tottenville and South Shore
  • Staten Island Mall area
