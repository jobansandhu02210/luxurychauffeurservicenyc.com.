Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our luxury chauffeur services, pricing, booking, and policies.

Pricing & Payment

Point-to-point is per mile: Business Sedan $8/mi, Business SUV $10/mi, First Class SUV $12/mi, First Class Sedan $16/mi. Hourly starts at $75/hr (Business Sedan, 3-hour minimum). Airport transfers have flat rates by airport and vehicle. Get a quote or call (212) 555-0199.

Gratuity is not included in our quoted prices. While tipping is not required, it is customary in the transportation industry. The standard gratuity is 15-20% of the fare. You can add gratuity when paying or provide cash directly to your chauffeur.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), corporate accounts with monthly billing, and cash. Payment is typically collected at the time of booking or at the completion of service, depending on your preference.

For airport transfers, tolls are typically included in the flat rate. For hourly and point-to-point services, tolls and parking fees are additional and will be itemized on your receipt. We always use E-ZPass for efficient toll payment.

Booking & Reservations

You can book through our website using the online reservation form, call us 24/7 at (212) 555-0199, or email info@luxurychauffeurservicenyc.com. For airport transfers, we recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance. Same-day bookings are available based on fleet availability.

Cancellations made 24+ hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a cancellation fee. For same-day cancellations, the full fare may be charged. Event and wedding bookings have specific cancellation terms outlined in the service agreement.

Yes, you can modify your reservation by calling us or contacting your account manager. We accommodate changes to pickup time, location, and vehicle type when possible. Changes within 2 hours of pickup may be subject to availability.

Yes, we offer corporate accounts with dedicated account managers, consolidated monthly billing, an online booking portal, custom reporting, and volume discounts. Contact us to set up a corporate account for your business.

Airport Services

We provide luxury car service to all major NYC area airports including JFK International Airport, LaGuardia Airport, Newark Liberty International Airport, Teterboro Airport (private aviation), and Westchester County Airport. All airport services include flight tracking and meet & greet.

Yes, we monitor all flights in real-time. If your flight is early or delayed, we automatically adjust your pickup time. For arriving passengers, we provide 60 minutes of complimentary wait time for international flights and 30 minutes for domestic flights.

For arrivals, your chauffeur will meet you in the arrivals area holding a sign with your name. We'll send you the chauffeur's contact information before your pickup so you can coordinate. For private aviation terminals, we meet you at the FBO.

Services & Fleet

We serve all five NYC boroughs (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, Bronx, Staten Island), Long Island, Westchester County, New Jersey, and Connecticut. We can also arrange long-distance transportation to destinations throughout the Northeast.

Our fleet includes executive sedans (Mercedes-Benz S-Class, BMW 7 Series), luxury SUVs (Cadillac Escalade ESV, Lincoln Navigator L), and executive Sprinter vans for groups up to 14 passengers. All vehicles are late-model, meticulously maintained, and detailed before each ride.
