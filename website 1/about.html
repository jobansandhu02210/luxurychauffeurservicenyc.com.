Our Story

Excellence in Motion Since 2010

Luxury Chauffeur Service NYC was founded with a simple mission: to provide the highest quality luxury transportation in New York City. What began as a small fleet serving a handful of corporate clients has grown into one of the city's most respected chauffeur services.

Our founder recognized that New York's busy professionals and discerning travelers deserved better than the inconsistent, impersonal transportation options available at the time. We set out to create a service that combined the reliability of a professional car service with the comfort and elegance of a private chauffeur.

Today, we're proud to serve Fortune 500 executives, celebrities, diplomats, and thousands of clients who simply appreciate exceptional service. But no matter how much we've grown, our commitment to personalized attention and uncompromising quality remains unchanged.

Every chauffeur undergoes a comprehensive vetting process that includes:

  • Extensive background checks and driving record verification
  • Drug and alcohol screening
  • Professional driving assessment
  • Customer service evaluation
  • NYC geography and navigation testing

Our chauffeurs participate in regular training programs covering:

  • Vehicle care and maintenance awareness
