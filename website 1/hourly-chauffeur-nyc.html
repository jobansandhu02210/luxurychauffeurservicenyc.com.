Private Chauffeur Hire by the Hour in NYC

Sometimes you need more than a single trip—you need a personal driver who's available whenever and wherever you need them. Our hourly chauffeur service gives you the flexibility to explore New York City on your own schedule with a professional driver and luxury vehicle at your disposal.

Whether you're planning a day of shopping on Fifth Avenue, attending multiple business meetings across Manhattan, or showing out-of-town guests the sights, our hourly service provides the convenience and comfort you deserve.

Perfect For

  • Shopping Excursions – Fifth Avenue, SoHo, Madison Avenue, and beyond
  • Business Meetings – Multiple stops across the city with efficient routing
  • City Tours – Show guests the best of NYC with a knowledgeable driver
  • Medical Appointments – Reliable transportation for healthcare visits
  • Real Estate Tours – View multiple properties in comfort and style
  • Day Trips – Explore the Hamptons, Hudson Valley, or Connecticut
  • Personal Errands – Your driver waits while you handle business

How Hourly Service Works

Booking hourly chauffeur service is simple. Choose your vehicle, select your start time and estimated duration, and let us know your pickup location. Your chauffeur arrives at the scheduled time and remains at your disposal for the entire booking period.

Need to extend your time? No problem—just let your chauffeur know, and we'll accommodate your schedule. With our hourly service, you're never rushed and never waiting.

What's Included

  • Professional Chauffeur – Experienced, courteous, and knowledgeable
  • Vehicle Choice – Business Sedan, Business SUV, First Class SUV, or First Class Sedan
  • Unlimited Stops – As many destinations as you need
  • Wait Time – Your driver waits at each stop
  • Complimentary Amenities – Bottled water, Wi-Fi, phone chargers
  • Flexible Scheduling – Extend or shorten as needed
